# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

Our community is dedicated to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for contributors of all backgrounds. We expect project participants to adhere to the following code of conduct.

Standards
- Be kind and respectful.
- Assume good intent and be constructive in criticism.
- Refrain from demeaning or exclusionary behavior and language.

Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances.

For full details, see: https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/0/code_of_conduct/
